Weight Maintain

Exercise Plans

Here are some exercises that can help you with weight maintenance:

  • Low-impact exercises like walking, yoga, or Pilates
  • Moderate-intensity exercises like jogging, cycling, or swimming
  • Resistance training exercises like weight lifting or resistance band workouts
    • Healthifyme

Diet Plans

Here are some diet plans that can help you with weight maintenance:

  • Balanced diets that include a variety of healthy foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats
  • Mediterranean diet, which emphasizes plant-based foods, healthy fats, and moderate amounts of lean protein
  • DASH (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) diet, which focuses on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and low-fat dairy
