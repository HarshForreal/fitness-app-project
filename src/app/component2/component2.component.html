Weight Gain

Exercise Plans

Here are some exercises that can help you with weight gain:

  • Compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, or bench press
  • Isolation exercises like bicep curls, tricep extensions, or leg curls
  • Functional exercises like pull-ups, push-ups, or lunges
  • Note: rest is also very important
    • Healthline

Diet Plans

Here are some diet plans that can help you with weight gain:

  • High-calorie diets like the GOMAD (Gallon of Milk a Day) diet
  • High-protein diets like the Dukan diet or the South Beach diet
  • Healthy weight gain diets with a balance of macronutrients like the Mediterranean diet
